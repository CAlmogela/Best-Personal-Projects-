My Story

Starting point of my journey

  • January 2011

    Humble Beginning

    Started out in life working as an Office Manager with Randstad F&A. I was humbled to be a part of a strong leadership team that valued employee development. My leadership team there equipt me with strong people skills that further morphed into strong Recruting skills.

  • August 2013

    Start of Sales Journey

    From there, I found myself in retail sales for T-Mobile. I worked up the ranks, mastered the art of instore sales and gained a position as Retail Store Manager. Then on I dedicated my time to developing my employees and helping them get further in their journey.

  • September 2019

    Transition to Small Business

    As time passed by, I found myself constantly seeking new challeneges, thus came Nakoshi. A small business, concived by my Brother in Law, catered to swag appareals. I joined the team as an Account Manager working towards building our pipleline from the ground up. Since then the jouney has been constantly growing towards many blessings.

  • Current

    Next Phase in Growth

    As I look toward the Horizion and the next goal, it's become inspired to build on top of my skills. While searching for new challenges, I had a chance to connect with a very smart individual that created OnDelta a marketing trade school. I have always been facinated by technology as well as building a business. Here at OnDelta, I've learned just that.
